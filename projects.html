Consultation Management Web Application

An application that allows students to sign up for consultations. They fill in a form asking for a consultation. The administrator has the option to log in and respond to consultation requests. He can accept them or propose a different consultation date, then an e-mail is sent to the MailTrap application. He also has the options to reject the consultation date and log out.

Technologies: PHPSlim, CakePHP, PHPMailer, MailTrap, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
